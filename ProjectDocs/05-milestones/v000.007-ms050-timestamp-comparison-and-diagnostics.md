## Processing Rules

### 1. Comparison Eligibility

Two candidates are comparable only if:

- they share a common representation type:
  - both UTC
  - or both timezone-aware (converted to UTC)
  - or both naive

Rules:

- UTC vs UTC â†’ comparable
- timezone-aware vs timezone-aware â†’ comparable via UTC conversion
- naive vs naive â†’ comparable
- any mixed representation â†’ NOT comparable

---

### 2. Comparison Value Selection

For comparison:

- UTC representation â†’ use ``utc_timestamp``
- timezone-aware â†’ convert to UTC and compare
- naive â†’ use ``naive_timestamp``

---

### 3. Comparison Execution

For each ordered pair (i < j):

- skip if same ``timestamp_source``
- skip if not comparable
- compare values
- record result

---

### 4. TimestampComparison Structure

Each comparison contains:

- ``left_source``
- ``right_source``
- ``representation`` (utc | naive)
- ``left_value``
- ``right_value``
- ``equal`` (boolean)

Representation rules:

- ``representation = "utc"`` when comparison is performed in UTC space
- ``representation = "utc"`` for:
  - UTC vs UTC comparisons
  - timezone-aware vs timezone-aware comparisons after UTC conversion
- ``representation = "naive"`` when comparison is performed using naive timestamps

---

### 5. Inconsistency Detection

A comparison is inconsistent if:

- the comparison is valid (i.e. representations are directly comparable)
- the two candidates originate from different timestamp sources
- the compared values are not equal

Only directly comparable pairs may be considered for inconsistency detection.

Non-comparable pairs must be ignored and must not produce inconsistencies.

---

### 6. Deterministic Ordering

Candidates must be sorted before comparison using:

1. ``timestamp_source``
2. comparison representation order:
   - ``utc`` (includes timezone-aware candidates compared via UTC)
   - ``naive``
3. ``naive_timestamp``
4. ``timezone_aware_timestamp`` (if present, otherwise empty)
5. ``utc_timestamp`` (if present, otherwise empty)

Comparison iteration must be deterministic:

- index-based (i < j)

---

### 7. No Side Effects

- Diagnostics must not:
  - modify candidates
  - affect resolution
  - influence output

---

### 8. No Implicit Behavior

- No timezone inference
- No cross-representation coercion
- No comparison of incompatible representations
