# Milestone — v000.006-ms036 — workflow-completeness

## Type

documentation

---

## Context

- Workflow may contain implicit steps or undefined transitions

---

## Goal

Define a complete workflow with explicit steps and transitions.

---

## Affected Documents

- ProjectDocs/development-workflow.md

---

## Definitions

- All workflow steps must be explicitly listed
- All transitions between steps must be explicitly defined
- No implicit steps allowed
- Workflow must be strictly sequential

---

## Invariants

- Existing validated rules must remain unchanged unless explicitly redefined
- No implicit transitions may be introduced
- All changes must preserve consistency with:
  - development-workflow.md
  - requirements
  - milestones overview

---

## Responsibilities

- Define complete workflow structure
- Eliminate implicit states

---

## Deliverables

- development-workflow.md contains:
  - complete list of steps
  - explicit transitions

---

## Constraints

- No runtime changes

---

## Validation Criteria

- All workflow steps are defined
- All transitions are defined
- No implicit states exist

---

## Non-Goals

- No changes to step ordering logic beyond completeness

---

## Covered Requirements

- R008 — Workflow Completeness
